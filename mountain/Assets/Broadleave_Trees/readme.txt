This package contains 16 different types of hi-detail broadleave trees.
Just drag the prefabs into your scene and arrange them as you like.

You may also want to have a look at the example scene that shows the different trees.

Also 8 exchangeable leave-textures are included, including bald-tree texture.

All single trees using about 2700-16000 Polygons.

The trees are not created with the Unity-tree-creator.

The original FBX-files are also included in the package.
